Chef GPT

Have you ever wanted to know what recipes you can make at home with the ingredients you already have? With “ChefGPT” you can input ingredients and not only will it find you the easiest recipes to make, it can do much more!

Tools Used

Slack, Google Drive, Miro, Figma

Roles, Deliverables & Timeline

Team of 3; Each person contributed to multiple roles such as Researcher, Designer, etc

Deliverables included were: Affinity Diagram, User Persona, Wireframe sketches, Low-Fidelity Prototype, Mid-Fidelity Clickable prototype

Timeline for deliverables was 3 Weeks with each week having multiple deliverables completed

Research

Conducted 1-on-1 user interviews via Zoom

- Synthesized data by noting down responses on sticky notes and grouping them accordingly in affinity diagram

- Created Google Forms survey for qualitative and quantitative Data

Definition & Synthesis & Ideation

Our target audience for this project were people who were trying to cook on a budget and reduce the amount of trips they need to take to the grocery store

We came up with the user scenario of our user persona, Patrick (Age 25, living alone) wanting to cook a quick meal with what he has. He inputs the ingredients he already has and finds out he'll need one more. From there Patrick decides to go to the grocery store to pick up the missing item and successfully cooks his meal
